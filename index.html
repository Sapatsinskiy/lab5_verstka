WAYGOOD is a meeting place for Richmond locals
(and everyone else). We’ve taken the best parts of
a bottle shop and a neighbourhood eatery and
mixed them together. Drop past for a drink, stay
for a bite or grab a bottle on the fly.

Our only rule is: come as
you are and enjoy as you
please. We look forward to
seeing you.
